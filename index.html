About us

Beginning its journey in 2008 with a group of young, motivated, highly skilled and energetic Alumni from IIT Roorkee, Occulus Designs Pvt Ltd came into being on 22nd October 2012.

Occulus as we fondly call it, is home to the finest people from IIT Roorkee .In a span of 12 years Occulus has established itself as a premium Engineering & Design Consulting company of the region under the capable mentorship of legends like Er.B S Gupta(Ex Deputy Director, CBRI) and Ar. Gajendra Mehta (Ex Deputy Director CBRI).

All this has become possible cause of the two things; the cordial and lasting relationship, which we could develop between the Client, Creator & Contractor and the professional excellence of the Occulus team that puts in their heart and soul into the design and execution of the given project.

The leadership team at Occulus has invested large amount of their time in developing the culture and systems of the company for designing the futuristic structures and giving the client a delightful experience of the services offered. Occulus is totally a hierarchy less and hurry less organization. The people at Occulus are given sufficient time to research, analyze, and experiment to develop the conceptual outlining for a particular project. It is because of the faith of our clients on our leadership that we are succeeding in designing the lasting structures translating into delightful experience for our clients and ourselves.Therefore, we are here to give our best for the people who worships design like us.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the qualification of people at Occulus?
The team at Occulus are highly qualified for the work they do, the leadership team have done their Graduation & Post Graduation from premier institutes like IIT Roorkee, PEC Chandigarh & premier institutes from Canada.The Chief Mentors have decades of research experience of CBRI Roorkee behind them. Therefore, you can be fully assured of the quality of services from Occulus.
If I plan to engage Occulus as consultant for my next project, who will design my project?
At Occulus we follow a systematic approach to design. Any project small or big which come to Occulus follows the same process and goes through stringent quality checks before it comes up for presentation to the client. So it is not one particular person rather the complete team that works on the project.
What are your charges?
Occulus is a premium Architectural design service firm. The consultancy charges are not same for every project. The charges vary from project to project depending upon the type of project, intensity of services involved and the amount of supervision involved with the project.
Do you make submission drawings also?
Yes we make submission drawings for projects we are giving consultancy.
Do you provide estimate of the buildings?
At Occulus we provide a complete gamut of services falling under the scope of Architectural Design like:
Literature Survey and Requirement formation
Site Selection, Feasibility Studies and Valuation
Concept Planning
Design, Working drawing, Estimation& Costing
Selection of Specifications of Materials
3D Modeling, Animation
Structural Designing
Interior Designing
Urban Designing
Landscape Designing
Site Supervision & Site Visits
Project Management Consultancy
Do you do turnkey projects?
Yes, we do turnkey projects also, provided they are of significant high value.
Do you recommend contractors?
We don’t force any contractor on the client. From our side the client is free to select the contractor of his choice. We recommend contractors only when the client asks us to do so.
